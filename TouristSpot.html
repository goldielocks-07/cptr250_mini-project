TOURIST SPOT OF VINTAR

UMOK NI SIWAWER

The Umok ni Siwawer is located in Brgy. 10 Parparorok, Sitio Bucana. Umok ni Siwawer Vintar Adventure offers thrilling adventure activities for adults and children. Push yourself to the limit with “The Bike Zipline”. Pedal your way as you view the river of Vintar Dam. Locals and tourists can experience this 230-meters long attraction at a very low cost. Other adventures included are zipline, surfline, superman zipline, 4x4 ATV, hydraucycle waterbike, floater, zurball, and kayak.

UBBOG TI ALSEM

Ubbog Ti Alsem is located in Brgy. 24 Alsem and is a popular spot for swimming and picnics. It is part of the Bislak River, offering cool and refreshing water where visitors can relax and enjoy nature. The area is surrounded by mountains, creating a peaceful and scenic environment. Ubbog Ti Alsem is perfect for families and friends looking to spend quality time outdoors while enjoying the natural beauty of Vintar.

ENCANTADORA FALLS

Encantadora Falls is in Brgy. 23, Dipilat and it is a favorite spot for swimming, cliff jumping, and picnics. The falls have clear water, making them perfect for fun with family and friends. Local farmers also use the water for plants. Surrounded by nature, Encantadora Falls is beautiful and relaxing.

SOLCOC FALLS

Vintar is very proud of SOLCOC FALLS located in Brgy. 30 Sto Tomas (Saricao) It's found in the heart of a virgin forest of Sitio Solcoc. Its main stream runs down through the Kaeltan River that connects to the other rivers (from Brgys Cabayo and Dagupan and with the Kadaklan River of Brgy Tamdagan). Its four layer falls showers very refreshing cool breeze.
